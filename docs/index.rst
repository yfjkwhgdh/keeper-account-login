How to Perform a Keeper Account Login
-------------------------------------

Follow these steps to sign in to your Keeper account:

1. Open the Keeper application or Web Vault.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Enter your Master Password.
4. Complete multi-factor authentication if enabled.
5. Access your encrypted Keeper vault.

Common Keeper Account Login Problems
------------------------------------

Incorrect Login Credentials
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Entering incorrect account details is one of the most common reasons for login failure.

Check the following:

* Confirm your email address.
* Enter your Master Password carefully.
* Check Caps Lock settings.
* Verify your keyboard language.

Internet Connection Issues
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

A weak connection may prevent successful authentication.

Try these solutions:

* Restart your internet connection.
* Switch to another network.
* Check your device connectivity.
* Temporarily disable VPN or proxy services.

Multi-Factor Authentication Errors
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

If authentication fails:

* Verify your security code.
* Check your authenticator application.
* Confirm your device time is correct.
* Use available backup verification options.

Browser or Extension Problems
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

If the Keeper browser extension is not working:

#. Restart your browser.
#. Update the extension.
#. Clear browser cache and cookies.
#. Reinstall the extension if required.

Outdated Keeper Application
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

An outdated application may cause login or synchronization issues.

Keep updated:

* Keeper applications.
* Web browsers.
* Operating systems.

Forgot Your Keeper Master Password?
-----------------------------------

The Master Password is required to unlock your encrypted Keeper vault.

If you cannot remember your password:

* Use available account recovery options.
* Follow Keeper's recovery instructions.
* Avoid repeated failed login attempts.

Password managers use strong security models, so your Master Password protection is an important part of account security.
